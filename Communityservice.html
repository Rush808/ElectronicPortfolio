Community Service

  • Assisted with community technology workshops to help bridge the digital divide

Featured Community Service

Beach Clean-Up Initiative

In Spring 2024, I organized and participated in a beach clean-up event at our local shoreline. Working with a team of 15 volunteers, we collected over 200 pounds of trash and recyclable materials.

This experience was incredibly rewarding as it allowed me to take direct action in preserving our natural environment while building connections with like-minded individuals in my community.

Date: April 15, 2024
Location: Local Beach
Hours Contributed: 8 hours
Role: Event Coordinator
